About Me

I am a software product manager and data scientist at the Air Quality Management District, a government agency that monitors air quality in the Bay Area. I love working with data and creating user friendly web apps and data visualizations for government employees to complete their work. I am driven by a fascination with intuitive interfaces and design. In my spare time, I host a web series called Common Sense TV that discusses current affairs and contemporary media and entertainment. I also ocassionally fiddle with creating wearable technology (a dress I worked on was exhibited in New York Fashion Week) and have previously launched a mobile fashion app called Stylematic as well as a citizen journalism app called Newsfly.
